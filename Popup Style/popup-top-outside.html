My Neck and Back Pain Was Killing Me! I Almost Lost My Job!

My neck, shoulder, and back pain were causing me to lose productivity. I was drowning in pain and stress – until I took matters into my own hands.

My career was on the line just two months ago. I had finally landed my dream job as a graphic designer for one of my favorite clothing brands. I spend hours creating visual concepts for the brand and expressing myself through my work. And the pay certainly wasn’t bad either!

But the long hours at my desk was giving me frequent headaches, neck, shoulder, and back pain – even when I wasn’t at work.

On top of the tight deadlines and a picky manager, I was beginning to doubt if I really belonged there.

One day, after nearly 12 hours at my desk, my coworker needed my opinion about a color scheme she was considering for a new logo.

When I turned around to talk to her, I felt my neck seize up and a sharp pain run down my back. My neck was completely stiff, and I decided it was probably time to call it a day. I was mentally and physically exhausted.

The next day I woke up still feeling a bit sluggish, but more so, defeated. The pain was unbearable.

When I got to the chiropractor, I waited about 45 minutes until it was my turn. Of course, there’s always a wait. I explained to the chiropractor about my neck and shoulder issues at work.
